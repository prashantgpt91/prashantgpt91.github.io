Why it matters

Routing everything to a flagship model has a price

And most of it never shows up as a line item until it's already a problem.

It costs you money

You pay frontier rates for work a fine-tuned 66M-parameter model does faster. Classification, extraction, sentiment, retrieval — none of it needs a 400-billion-parameter model, yet every call is billed as if it did.

It costs you sovereignty

Every request leaves your perimeter. Customer PII, financial records, and PHI flow into a third party's infrastructure — frequently across a border you're contractually bound to respect.

It costs you control

Your most critical workflows inherit one vendor's pricing, limits, and outages. Switching costs compound with every workload you add, and their roadmap quietly becomes yours.

The control plane

One layer that decides where every workload runs

Five capabilities between your applications and every engine they call.

Routing

Workload Router

Decomposes each request and sends every step to the optimal engine — by cost, latency, accuracy, and policy. The intelligence that turns a pile of models into a system.

Policy

Policy Engine

Declarative rules for where workloads may run — by region, vendor, data class, or team.

Governance

Governance Layer

Centralized security, RBAC, and audit trails across every model and every call.

Cost

Cost Optimizer

Continuously shifts work to the cheapest engine that meets the bar — and proves the savings.

Observability

Observability Platform

Live visibility into cost, latency, routing decisions, and performance — per team and workflow.
